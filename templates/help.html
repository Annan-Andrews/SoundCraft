Supported Audio Formats

The following audio formats are supported for conversion:

  • .wav (Waveform Audio Format)
  • .mp3 (MPEG Audio Layer III)
  • .ogg (Ogg Vorbis)

How to Use Audio to Text Conversion?

To convert audio to text, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the "Audio to Text" page.
  2. Click on the "Upload Audio File" button.
  3. Select the audio file (supported formats: .wav, .mp3, .ogg).
  4. Click the "Convert Audio to Text" button.
  5. Wait for the conversion process to finish.
  6. The converted text will be displayed on the page.

How to Use Text to Audio Conversion?

To convert text to audio, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the "Text to Audio" page.
  2. Enter the text you want to convert to audio in the provided field.
  3. Click the "Convert Text to Audio" button.
  4. Wait for the conversion process to finish.
  5. You can download the converted audio file.

How to Use Real-Time Speech Recognition?

To use real-time speech recognition, follow these steps:

  1. Visit the "Real-Time Speech Recognition" page.
  2. Click the "Start Recording" button and speak.
  3. The recognized text will be displayed as you speak.
  4. Click the "Stop Recording" button to end the recognition.

FAQs

Q: What if my uploaded audio file is not in the supported formats?

A: If your audio file is not in .wav, .mp3, or .ogg format, the conversion process may fail. Please ensure your file is in one of these formats.

Q: Can I convert large audio files?

A: The conversion process works best with reasonably sized audio files. Large files may take longer to process and may encounter issues.

Q: How do I download the converted audio file?

A: After converting text to audio, you will see a download button next to the audio preview. Click on it to download the audio file to your device.

Q: How accurate is the speech recognition?

A: The accuracy of speech recognition depends on various factors such as audio quality, background noise, and clarity of speech. For best results, record clear and concise speech.

Q: Is there a limit on the length of audio files I can convert?

A: There is no strict limit, but longer audio files may take more time to convert and may require stable internet connectivity.

Q: How can I report issues or provide feedback?

A: If you encounter any issues or have feedback to share, please contact us at support@soundcraft.com. We appreciate your input!
